How the Insta demo works

Step 1
Create an account & channel

Choose a username, set a channel name and (optionally) a profile picture. All of this is written to localStorage under your browser.

Step 2
Upload videos & audio files

Pick a file from your device, add a title, description and category. Videos can be regular or Shorts; audio files go to InstaMusic.

Step 3
Browse, subscribe, experiment

Watch your own uploads, switch accounts, subscribe to channels and check views/likes/comments ‚Äì all without any real backend or network calls.
